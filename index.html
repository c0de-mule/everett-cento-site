About

Technology should serve the work, not complicate it.

There's something exciting about building with AI, so exciting that it's easy to lose sight of the outcome in pursuit of the tool. But the best solutions usually start with a simpler question: what's the smallest change that will make the biggest difference? Start there, and let the results tell you whether you need more.

The same restraint applies to the tools themselves. My background in Media Studies taught me how systems shape the messages that move through them—and AI is no different. These tools should adapt to your voice and your workflow, not flatten them into something generic. That's the work I care about: helping you do more with less, without sacrificing what makes your work unique.

AI Automation Marketing Agency

Podcast Content Pipeline

A podcast studio publishing episodes weekly needed a faster way to turn raw recordings into polished, platform-ready content—without adding hours of manual work after every episode.

Built an automated pipeline entirely in Claude Code — using transcript parsing and structured output prompting to generate YouTube descriptions, show notes, social captions, and chapter markers. Formatted and ready to publish.

Result

What used to take hours of post-production writing now takes minutes. Every episode ships with consistent, on-brand content across every platform.
