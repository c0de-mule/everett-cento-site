About

Technology should serve the work, not complicate it.

There's something exciting about building with AI, so exciting that it's easy to lose sight of the outcome in pursuit of the tool. But the best solutions usually start with a simpler question: what's the smallest change that will make the biggest difference? Start there, and let the results tell you whether you need more.

The same restraint applies to the tools themselves. My background in Media Studies taught me how systems shape the messages that move through them—and AI is no different. These tools should adapt to your voice and your workflow, not flatten them into something generic. That's the work I care about: helping you do more with less, without sacrificing what makes your work unique.

AI Automation Health & Wellness

Community Management GPT

Social media reps were spending hours crafting responses—and still getting the brand voice wrong on specialized wellness questions.

Built a custom GPT trained on brand voice samples and technique documentation, with guardrails to ensure accuracy on sensitive health-related content.

Result

Enabled response to nearly every comment and DM. Consistent, on-brand answers that actively qualify leads and drive class signups.

AI Automation Agency

Email Intelligence System

Founder drowning in 500+ daily emails needed inbox control without adding another SaaS subscription.

Built entirely in Google Apps Script with Gemini API: auto-sorting by client and newsletter, urgent message tagging, Slack alerts for high-volume clients, and daily digests with meeting context pulled from Calendar and email threads.

Result

10+ clients accurately sorted. Surfaced meeting context automatically. Urgent requests now visible via Slack instead of buried in inbox.
